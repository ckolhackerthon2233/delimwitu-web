## 🖼️ Image Requirements
- **Format**: PNG or JPG (preferred)
- **Size**: Minimum 400x300px, ideally 800x600px
- **Quality**: High resolution, well-lit, appetizing photos
- **Naming**: Use kebab-case (lowercase with hyphens)
  - ✅ `mango-juice.png`
  - ✅ `coke-300.png`
  - ✅ `jolof-beef-full.png`
  - ❌ `Mango Juice.png`
  - ❌ `coke_300.jpg`
